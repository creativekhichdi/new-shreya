Roorkee Manufacturing Unit

Roorkee, known for Asia’s oldest Engineering College (Thompson College, now an IIT) and home to the oldest army cantonment is the second centre for Shreya Life Sciences’ production unit.
Established in 2008, this plant too is certified by WHO for Bulk, Sterile & Oral Solid Products. It is also certified by GMP.
Commissioned as per the guidelines of US FDA & UK MHRA (MCA), the unit caters to all domestic and export requirements.
The Roorkee production plant has a separate manufacturing block for each product category. Soon, this plant is also set to have a state-of-the-art Insulin formulation unit.
