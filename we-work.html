How we work

Domestic Sales & Marketing

At Shreya Life Science, there exists an extensive Sales and Marketing set up. At the domestic level, the Shreya Group markets through two separate SBUs.

  • Amadeus

  • AkuBio

The fleet of field personnel collectively work in collaboration with over 200,000 doctors specialising in all major medical segments. These fields include:

  • General Medicine

  • General Surgery

  • Diabetology

  • Cardiology

  • General Practice

  • Gynaecology

  • Paediatrics

  • Gastroenterology

  • Ophthalmology

  • Oncology

The Marketing operations are handled by a dynamic fleet of over 1000 Sales & Marketing associates. Along with the extensive effort of field personnel, They are equipped with a full-fledged back-end support system. This team comprises of the following:

  • Sales Management team at the Corporate and Regional levels

  • Marketing Management Team at Corporate level

  • Training Department both at the Corporate & Field levels

  • Medical & Regulatory Cell

  • Market Research

  • InfoTech Department

Exports

At Shreya Life Sciences, the utmost priorities for exporting pharmaceutical products are driven by providing trusted, reliable, safe and high-quality products to improve the health and well-being of Patients.
Currently, SHREYA exports a wide range of formulations to various countries across five continents.

Key highlights of SHREYA’ International Exports include:

  • High export volumes to Russia & CIS Countries.

  • Export of in-house products to African and Far-East countries.

  • Many Shreya’s products are either registered or under registration in many of the countries.

Our Entire Scope of International Exports

Russia

- Operations are set up across the country.
- Products are exported for both Prescription & OTC Segments.
- Over 55 products are registered & marked by over 160 field force.
- Caters to 25000 retail pharmacies (APTEKAS).
- Products also distributed through the Public Distribution System of the Russian Govt.

Africa

- Shreya Life Sciences began operations in this continent through its first entry into Ethiopia.
- Current operations include Kenya, Uganda, DRC Congo & Ethiopia.
- Approval to establish plants in Nigeria is currently under process.
- Our Lozenges is under registration at Morocco.

Kazakhstan

- Partnership with ASNA: a large chain of chemist stores for product marketing with over 3000 retail shops in the country.
- 15 products of Shreya Life Sciences are under registration and expected to start operations soon.

Latin America

- Operations in Cuba, Ecuador, Columbia & Dominican Republic.
- Four products in Ecuador and one product In Cuba are currently under registration.

Uzbekistan

- 35 field staff on ground currently promoting 21 products.
- 5 products of Shreya Life Sciences are under registration in the country.

Myanmar

- 24 products under the label of Shreya are registered.
- Contract Manufacturing for SUN Pharma for sale in Myanmar

Papua New Guinea

- Insulin (Recosulin Brand) is exported to Papua New Guinea.

Contract Manufacturing:

Armed with more than 20+ years of industry expertise, Shreya Life Sciences, collaborates with Pharmaceutical Marketing firms for contract manufacture basis to produce and supply Formulations.

State-of-the-art manufacturing facility for oral solids at Aurangabad has PICS approval

Currently, illustrious portfolio of clients who have recognised SHREYA include globally recognised firms like Lupin Limited, Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd , Cadila Pharmceuticals Ltd , Piramal Pharma, Glenmark Pharmaceuticals & others.

Shreya welcomes contract manufacturing enquiries from both, domestic and international clients.

Kindly send your enquiries to: contractmanufacture@shreya.co.in
