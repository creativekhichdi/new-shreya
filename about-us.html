About Us

The best of pharmaceutical expertise for the betterment of humankind

Shreya Life Sciences is a state-of-the-art pharmaceutical organisation involved in research, manufacturing and distribution of medicinal formulations and products.

Beginning its journey in 1995 in Russia, Shreya Life Sciences started as a pharmaceutical distributing and marketing company, in the course of over two decades, Shreya Life Sciences has pioneered innovations and achieved milestones in acquiring new companies, commissioning new products as well as expanding its markets (both Domestic & Overseas) & also venturing in contract manufacturing activities. Now its presence and reach are in Russia, CIS countries, South East Asia, Africa, and Latin America

Success at SHREYA comes from distinguished expertise, processes, and partners in developing formulations and paving the way forward. All stake holders at Shreya, be it suppliers, clients or distributors are considered as a part of Large SHREYA Family and grow together.
